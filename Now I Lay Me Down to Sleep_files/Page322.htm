Quinton wasn't sure about this at all. He wiped at the lingering droplets of water dripping from his hair and down his forehead. He'd taken a hot shower, like Dr. Melody had suggested. He pulled on flannel cotton pajama bottoms and turned to flip off the bedroom light, making his way carefully towards his bed in the darkness. He sat on the bed and pulled the covers around him, settling in. What could it hurt, though? Closing his eyes, he began to repeat the mantra he'd learned in counseling that day.

 

Now I lay me down to sleep…
